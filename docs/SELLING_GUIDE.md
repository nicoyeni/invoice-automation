# Selling Guide: Invoice Processing Automation

## The Pitch (30 seconds)

> "You know that person on your team who spends hours every week opening invoice
> emails, typing numbers into spreadsheets, and chasing down late payments?
> I built a system that does all of that automatically — it reads invoices,
> pulls out the data, puts it in your spreadsheet, and even reminds you
> when payments are due. It costs less than one day of that employee's salary
> per month, and it never makes typos."

## Who To Target

### Best Fit Clients
- **10-100 employees** — big enough to have invoice volume, small enough to not have dedicated AP software
- **Service businesses** — agencies, consultants, contractors (lots of vendor invoices)
- **Property management** — multiple vendors per property, tons of recurring invoices
- **Small retail/e-commerce** — supplier invoices
- **Construction** — subcontractor invoices
- **Medical/dental practices** — supply vendor invoices

### Red Flags (Skip These)
- Already using QuickBooks with auto-import (they're partially solved)
- Less than 20 invoices/month (not worth it for them)
- Highly regulated (banks, government) — compliance overhead not worth it at this stage

## How To Find Them

1. **Local networking** — chamber of commerce, BNI groups, Rotary clubs
2. **LinkedIn outreach** — search for "office manager" or "bookkeeper" at small companies
3. **Accountant partnerships** — find local bookkeepers/CPAs, offer referral fees
4. **Cold email** — short, specific, include a demo video

## Pricing Model

### Option A: Project + Retainer (Recommended to start)
- **Setup fee**: $1,500 - $3,000
  - Covers: Google Cloud setup, config, testing with their real invoices, training
- **Monthly retainer**: $200 - $500/month
  - Covers: hosting, monitoring, updates, support
  - Your actual costs: ~$15-30/month

### Option B: Per-Invoice Pricing
- $1 - $3 per invoice processed
- Works for high-volume clients (200+ invoices/month)
- Easier to justify ROI: "each invoice costs you $5-10 in employee time"

### Option C: Value-Based (For larger clients)
- Calculate their current cost: (hours/week on AP) × (hourly rate) × 4
- Price at 30-50% of that number
- Example: 10 hrs/week × $25/hr × 4 = $1,000/month → charge $400/month

## The Demo

1. Ask the prospect to email you 3 real invoices (different vendors)
2. Run them through the system in front of them
3. Show the Google Sheet output with all data extracted
4. Show the anomaly flags
5. Total demo time: 5-10 minutes

**This is your killer move.** Nothing sells like seeing their own invoices
processed in real-time.

## Objections & Responses

**"What if it makes a mistake?"**
> Every invoice gets a confidence score. Anything below 70% gets flagged for
> human review. You're not removing the human — you're removing the boring
> parts so they can focus on the exceptions.

**"We already use QuickBooks/Xero"**
> Great — this feeds directly into your existing spreadsheet or we can adapt
> it to push into QuickBooks. It's the intake step that's manual, not the
> accounting software itself.

**"Is our data safe?"**
> Your invoices are processed by Google's AI (Gemini) under their enterprise
> security. Nothing is stored permanently — it's read, extracted, and the
> original stays in your email/Drive. I can show you the privacy policy.

**"Can't we just use ChatGPT?"**
> You could upload one invoice at a time to ChatGPT. This system watches
> your email 24/7, processes automatically, writes to your spreadsheet,
> checks for duplicates, and sends payment reminders. It's the automation
> around the AI that creates the value.

## Upsell Path

Once they're happy with invoice processing:
1. **Expense reports** — same tech, different document type (+$100-200/month)
2. **Receipt scanning** — photo → categorized expense (+$100/month)
3. **Payment reminders** — automated AP follow-ups (+$100/month)
4. **Monthly financial summaries** — AI-generated report from their data (+$200/month)
5. **Multi-location** — if they have branches, replicate per location
